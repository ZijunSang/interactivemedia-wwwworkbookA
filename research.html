---🌟 Monolith Project 🌟--- Nando Correa

The Monolith Project, inspired by 2001: A Space Odyssey, offers an immersive cosmic journey through scroll-based interactive art. It perfectly aligns with my theme of the universe and consciousness, turning abstract ideas of awakening into an engaging experience. This work made me realize how breaking cognitive boundaries relates to self-awakening and how immersive visuals strengthen grand philosophical ideas. Click to Visit site!
